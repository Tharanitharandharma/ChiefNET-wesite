Background of our company

Our company is an Enterprise Digital Engineering company, combining deep technical expertise and industry experience to help our customers with solutions with measurable business value and beyond. We leverage the open source tools and technologies and combine it with proprietary software to create meaningful value for all stake holders.

Our company was established in 1981 to provide sales and services to a new and rising technology industry. Personal Computers (PCs) were new to small business and the knowledge and expertise was few and far between.

Many computer stores could just not provide the necessary services and hands-on support small business desperately needed.

Great connections start with great people. We’re committed to ensuring a collaborative and nurturing culture, building a diverse and inclusive workforce and providing benefits
that make our employees’ lives better.



As network engineers, we do a lot of research. We have to figure out how networking protocols work and how to configure network devices.

The role can include designing system configurations, documenting and managing the installation of a new network, and maintaining and upgrading existing systems as necessary

What is ChiefNET?

ChiefNet is a revolutionary cloud native networking solution that provides the enterprise interconnecting framework to securely connect offices, remote workers, IOT devices and datacenters in hybrid multi-cloud environment. With an innovatively architected cloud orchestration platform, ChiefNet enables completely software defined enterprise WAN for SME to large organizations
